## ðŸ” **KEY FINDINGS**

### **1. Balanced Offense + Defense > Pure Offense**
- **Bruiser** (99.7%) beats **Pure Attack** (46%)
- Having some defense makes you much more durable
- 200 HP + 15 defense >> 100 HP + 5 defense

### **2. HP Stacking is Very Strong**
- **HP Tank** with 500 HP has 89.2% win rate
- Massive HP pool outlasts most builds
- Only really loses to Bruiser (more balanced stats)

### **3. Speed/Dodge Builds Underperform**
- **Speed Demon** (24.3%) and **Dodge Tank** (43.1%) are weak
- Despite high dodge %, not enough damage to win
- Doesn't compensate for lack of HP/defense

### **4. Pure Attack is Mediocre**
- **Pure Attack** has only 46% win rate
- Glass cannon without the speed/crit to burst
- Needs defense or HP to survive

### **5. Spell Power is Currently Useless**
- **Spell Caster** has 0% win rate
- Spell power doesn't affect basic attacks in simulation
- Would need ability damage to be viable

### **6. Critical Strike Needs Support Stats**
- **Crit Build** (31.4%) doesn't perform well alone
- Needs attack power AND crit chance together
- **Glass Cannon** does this better (55 attack + 30% crit)

### **7. Balanced Builds are Viable**
- **Balanced** has 62.8% win rate
- Not the best, but solid all-around
- Good for beginners
